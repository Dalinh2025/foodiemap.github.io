About Us

Our purpose is to help food enthusiasts find the best places to eat across Cambodia with ease and speed. Whether you're craving traditional Khmer dishes, street food, or international cuisine, Foodie Map offers an intuitive platform where users can search for restaurants, cafes, and food stalls based on location, cuisine type, ratings, and more. With detailed reviews, photos, and menus, it ensure that every user can make informed decisions quickly. The website also features interactive maps, so you can easily navigate your way to the top eateries in Phnom Penh. Whether you're a local or a traveler, Foodie Map makes your dining experience smoother and more enjoyable!
